Workshops

Call for Workshops

CNSM 2023 invites proposals for one-day / half-day workshops, which will be held on 30 October. The main purpose of the CNSM 2023 workshops is to provide fora for research, development, integration, standards, service provisioning, and user communities addressing timely topics relevant to the main conference.

Visit the Call for Workshops page to find out more details, if you are interested in creating a workshop proposal.

Accepted Workshops

AnServApp 2023

3rd International Workshop on Analytics for Service and Application Management

The main goal of AnServApp is to present research and work-in-progress results in the area of data analytics, machine learning and cognitive science for service and application management. Approaches such as predictive data analytics, data mining, machine learning and deep learning are promising mechanisms to harness this immense stream of service and application data to meet the needs of an organization.

See more details on AnServApp

Paper submission for AnServApp 2023 through EDAS using this link.

Block 6G

1st Workshop on Blockchain-enabled Network Management and Orchestration in 6G Era (BLOCK-6G)

BLOCK-6G workshop aims to provide a platform for researchers, industry professionals, and academics to explore the intersection of network management, orchestration, and blockchain technology in the 6G era.

See more details on Block 6G

Paper submission for Block 6G through EDAS using this link.

Continuum Internet and 6G

Workshop on Continuum Internet and 6G (CI-6G 2023)

Continuum Internet aims to highlight recent research related to (i) architectures and frameworks for Continuum Internet, (ii) effective Management and Operations for Continuum Internet, (iii) enhanced Communication, Computing and Network Integrated Technologies and 6G for Interconnected and Interworking of Smart Devices across Continuum Internet and (iv) novel use-cases and testbeds relevant to Continuum Internet and 6G.

See more details on Continuum Internet

Paper submission for Continuum Internet through EDAS .

XAI4Network

The First International Workshop on Explainable AI for Network Management and Future Internet (XAI4Network-2023)

XAI4Network aims to bring together computer scientists and engineers in different disciplines to share and exchange their experience and ideas and discuss state-of-the-art and in-progress research on all aspects of explainable AI for network management and future Internet.

See more details on XAI4Network
